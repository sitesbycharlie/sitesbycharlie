Privacy Policy.

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

This website is operated by Sites By Charlie, a web design service based in Dublin, Ireland. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at sitesbycharlie@gmail.com.

What data we collect

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide through the contact form on this website. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your mobile number (optional)
  • Any message or project details you choose to share

We do not collect any data automatically. This website uses no analytics tools, tracking pixels, advertising cookies, or any other passive data collection.

How we use your data

The information you submit via the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry and communicate with you about your project. We will never use your details for unsolicited marketing, and we will never sell or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

How your data is stored

Contact form submissions are processed and stored by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. Formspree stores your submission on their servers in order to deliver it to us. You can read Formspree's own privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not operate our own database or server-side storage of your personal information.

Cookies

This website does not set any first-party cookies. Formspree may set a functional cookie in order to process contact form submissions — this is strictly necessary for the form to operate and does not track you across other websites.

Your rights under GDPR

As someone based in or dealing with businesses in Ireland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • The right to access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • The right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your personal data
  • The right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to object — you can object to how we process your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at sitesbycharlie@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. We recommend checking back occasionally if you have an ongoing relationship with us.

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests relating to your personal data, please get in touch: sitesbycharlie@gmail.com
